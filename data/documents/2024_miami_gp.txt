The 2024 Miami Grand Prix was a historic event as Lando Norris claimed his maiden Formula 1 victory for McLaren. After struggling with tire degradation early in the season, McLaren brought a massive upgrade package to Miami. During the race, a well-timed Safety Car allowed Norris to pit and come out ahead of Max Verstappen. Verstappen, dealing with floor damage after hitting a bollard, couldn't match Norris's pace on the restart. Norris pulled away to win by over 7 seconds, becoming the first driver other than Carlos Sainz to beat Red Bull in over a year. Analysts praised McLaren's strategic patience and the sheer aerodynamic efficiency of the new floor upgrades.
